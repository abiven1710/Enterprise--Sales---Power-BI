Enterprise Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard
===========================================

Dashboard Overview
------------------
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of enterprise-level retail sales performance.  
It was developed using Power BI to track revenue, profit, growth, orders, and target achievement across regions, product categories, and states.  
The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by offering clear insights into sales trends and business performance.

Tools and Techniques Used
-------------------------
Power BI
- Power Query for data cleaning and transformation
- Data modeling and relationships
- DAX measures and calculated columns
- KPI cards and interactive visuals
- Filters, slicers, and drill-down features
- Map and regional analysis visuals

Dataset Summary
---------------
The dataset includes retail sales data covering the last 12 months with the following attributes:
- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Orders
- Year-over-Year Growth
- Sales Target
- Region
- State
- Product Category
- Time Period

Key Business Metrics
-------------------
- Total Revenue: $12,658,152
- Total Profit: $2,569,875
- Profit Margin: 20.3 percent
- Total Orders: 51,479
- Year-over-Year Growth: 14.2 percent
- Sales vs Target Achievement: 96.5 percent

Detailed Analysis and Insights
------------------------------

Monthly Revenue Trend  
- Revenue shows a consistent upward trend over the last 12 months  
- Peak monthly revenue reached approximately $1.36M  
- Growth indicates strong overall business performance  

Revenue by Region  
- US West generates the highest revenue at $4.38M  
- US East and US South show stable and balanced performance  
- US Central contributes the lowest revenue among regions  

Revenue by Product Category  
- Technology is the top-performing category contributing approximately 45 percent of total revenue  
- Furniture contributes around 26 percent  
- Office Supplies account for the remaining share  

Sales vs Target Analysis  
- Overall sales achievement stands at 96.5 percent of the target  
- Technology category performs closest to the target  
- Other categories show opportunities for improvement  

Sales by State  
- Texas records the highest sales at approximately $1.52M  
- California and New York follow as strong contributors  
- Geographic distribution highlights regional sales concentration  

Business Performance Trends  
---------------------------
- Profit growth aligns closely with revenue growth  
- Stable profit margin indicates effective cost management  
- Year-over-year growth reflects positive business momentum  

Recommendations
---------------
- Focus on high-performing regions such as US West for expansion  
- Improve performance in underperforming regions through targeted strategies  
- Invest more in the Technology category to maximize revenue  
- Monitor categories below target and adjust pricing or promotions  
- Use state-level insights to optimize regional sales planning  

Conclusion
----------
The Enterprise Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI provides clear visibility into sales performance, profitability, and growth trends.  
It demonstrates effective use of data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visuals to support strategic business decisions.
